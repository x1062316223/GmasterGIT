Can you survive The Long Dark?
The Long Dark is a thoughtful, exploration-survival experience that challenges solo players to think for themselves as they explore and expansive frozen wilderness in the aftermath of a geomagnetic disaster. There are no zombies - only you, the cold, and all the threats Mother Nature can muster.

Deep survival simulation gameplay where every decision matters.
Explore a large open world in search of resources.
Two Game Modes: Survival, Episodic Story Mode "Wintermute"
